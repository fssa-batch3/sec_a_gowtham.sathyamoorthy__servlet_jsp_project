Our story

Started in 2020 , by Settu Foundation

The inspiration came from severe parking crunch we faced at our offices.
While at the same a number of people would wait at the reception to get the
company cab home. A number of employees were also travelling to and from the same route
or destination, but were hesitant to offer rides. It was then, that the idea struck us,
to develop a fully automated way for employees to carpool.
